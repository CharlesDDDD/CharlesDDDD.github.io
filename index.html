Now I vote for Large Language models :). Specifically, I am currently or potentially interested in the following aspects of LLMs:

  • Reasoning Ability: How to make language models less prone to hallucination? What's the relationship between memorization and reasoning?
  • Tool-augmented LLMs: How to teach language models to use tools (to overcome the limitation of itself)? I believe learning how to use tools is a necessary condition for AGI.
  • Applications and Analysis: How to make benchmarks more fair? How to detect contamination in the current benchmark? How to build pipeline to make people more convenient to AI tools?
