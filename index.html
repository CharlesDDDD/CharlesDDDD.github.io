Research

I'm interested in exploring the challenges and opportunities presented by multilingual NLP, including the development of algorithms and models that can effectively handle multiple languages. I am also interested in exploring the ways in NLP for social good, such as improving access to information for marginalized communities or addressing issues related to bias and fairness in language technologies.
